About Me

I am a London-based Data Scientist and Python Developer with a strong focus on transforming data into clear, practical insights. I work across the full data workflow—from data cleaning and exploratory analysis to visualisation and model development—with an emphasis on well-structured, reliable solutions.

I have practical experience using Python for data analysis, automation, and problem-solving, supported by a solid foundation in statistics and data science principles developed through formal training, including an online Data Science programme with Imperial College London. I am particularly interested in applying data-driven thinking to real-world problems, uncovering meaningful patterns, and communicating findings in a clear and accessible way.

Alongside my technical work, I bring a thoughtful design mindset and a background in photography, which influences how I approach data visualisation and presentation. I value clarity, simplicity, and usability, whether I am writing code, analysing data, or presenting results.
